Know everything about your Drupal sites.

Dewy gives you a complete understanding of your Drupal sites, allowing for easier maintenance than you ever thought possible.

See it all

Bring your sites into Dewy and discover what sites are big or small, active or stale, loved or needing love. Helpful views bring your sites into focus, whether you have 5 sites or 500 sites.

Powerful querying

Ever been in a meeting where someone asked how many Drupal sites are live with webforms? Version 3.24? And average over 1000 hits a day? Any question, no matter how complex, can be answered with a few clicks.

Site notifications

Let your sites tell you when they need your attention. A site administrator turned off caching on a production site? Dewy will notify you.

Project notifications

Know the moment your sites are insecure. Vital project updates come to you as soon as they are released on Drupal.org.

Coming soon:
Mailing lists, integrations and more!

Create mailing lists of users. Talk to the Dewy API to create unique workflows. Powerful new features are on the way.
